What is CollegeNut?

CollegeNut is a application I made in order for high school students to better understand the college application process. So far the functionality is not as sophisticated as I want it to be and there are a finite number of high schools and colleges, but I plan to improve this as time goes on. My future aspirations for this project include having day in the life videos that give students a look into future careers and provide resources for alternatives to college. Information for colleges and high schools are taken from reputable resources such as CollegeData.com and niche.com.


Account

The Account section will contain your username and your other relevant account information. You can update all information except the username. Try to fill the satErwb, satMath, ACT sections since these attributes will provide a more accurate college search. Your favorited colleges will also be here as well.


High School

Choose a high school provided by the drop down menu. This will show relevant information for the chosen high school. Right now there are about 106 high schools in the US accounted for , this will be expanded on in the future.


College

Search for colleges by name. The search doesn't need specific college names. For example, putting in "c" will yield all the colleges that start with the letter "c". The colleges in the results will come with information related to each. You can choose to favorites some colleges. Colleges will also come with a compatibility percentage, which will tell students how similar they are to the average student in that college based on APS taken, SAT scores , and ACT scores. By default colleges with the highest compatibility are shown first but this can be changed with the increasing checkbox.
